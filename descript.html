Descript – Edit audio & video by editing text

Podcast editing · Video editing · AI voice

Descript turns your audio and video into editable text. You cut mistakes, remove filler words, and rearrange content simply by editing the transcript.[web:242][web:248]

It also offers AI features like overdub voice, studio‑quality audio cleanup, and automatic captioning.

Key strengths

  • Text‑based editing: Edit podcasts and talking‑head videos the same way you edit a document.[web:242]
  • Audio cleanup: Remove noise and improve clarity with one‑click effects.
  • Captions & repurposing: Generate captions and short clips for social media.

Best use cases

  • Podcast editing for solo creators and small teams.
  • Talking‑head YouTube videos, interviews, and tutorials.
  • Repurposing long recordings into short clips and audiograms.

Simple workflow

  1. Import your recording; Descript automatically transcribes it.
  2. Delete mistakes and filler words in the text – the audio/video updates instantly.
  3. Add music, images, and captions, then export your final video.
