Runway – AI‑powered video editing

Video editing · Background removal · Generative video

Runway is a browser‑based video editor with built‑in AI features such as background removal, text‑to‑video, motion tracking, and stylization.[web:242][web:248]

It helps creators turn raw footage into polished clips without complex timelines or heavy desktop software.

Visit Runway

Key strengths

  • Web‑based: No heavy installs; edit from almost any machine with a browser.
  • Smart tools: Background removal, object masking, and text‑to‑video for creative shots.[web:242]
  • Creator‑friendly UI: Simple controls compared with traditional NLEs.

Best use cases

  • Creating fast, stylish B‑roll for YouTube and ads.
  • Removing backgrounds for talking‑head videos without a green screen.
  • Applying unique looks or AI‑generated scenes to client projects.

Simple workflow

  1. Upload your footage to a Runway project.
  2. Use AI tools (background removal, motion tracking, stylization) on key clips.
  3. Arrange cuts on the timeline, then export for YouTube or social platforms.

Strengths: simple UI, AI tools

Limitations: cloud‑based, exports use credits
