Sora 2 – Next‑generation text‑to‑video

Video generation · Concept visuals · Ads

Sora 2 is a text‑to‑video model that generates realistic scenes, camera moves, and lighting directly from written prompts or storyboards.[web:247][web:259]

It is designed to help creators quickly visualize ideas for ads, branded content, and cinematic sequences without a full production crew.

Learn about Sora 2

Key strengths

  • Cinematic results: Advanced motion, depth, and lighting suitable for concept trailers and ad ideas.[web:256]
  • Scene consistency: Maintains subjects and environments across multiple seconds of footage.[web:247]
  • Prompt‑driven control: You can describe camera moves, mood, and style in detail.[web:247]

Best use cases

  • Creating visual concepts for ad campaigns before full production.
  • Generating background footage and B‑roll for explainer videos.
  • Pitching ideas to clients with quick video mockups.

Simple workflow

  1. Write a short script or scene description with subject, motion, and style.
  2. Generate a clip, review it, then refine your prompt for better control.
  3. Combine multiple Sora clips in an editor to build a full sequence.

Strengths: realism, motion, storytelling

Limitations: early access, needs careful prompts
