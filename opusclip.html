OpusClip – Auto‑create short clips from long videos

Short‑form content · TikTok · Reels

OpusClip analyzes long videos and automatically finds the most engaging moments, turning them into vertical clips with captions and dynamic zooms.[web:242][web:248]

It helps you repurpose podcasts, interviews, and long‑form content into multiple shorts for TikTok, Reels, and YouTube Shorts.

Visit OpusClip

Key strengths

  • Highlight detection: AI scores segments based on hooks, pacing, and key moments.[web:248]
  • Automatic captions: Adds stylized captions optimized for vertical content.
  • Batch output: Generates multiple clips from one long video.

Best use cases

  • Turning podcasts into a week of TikTok / Reels clips.
  • Clipping webinars and live streams into highlight reels.
  • Producing short educational snippets from long tutorials.

Simple workflow

  1. Upload a long video or paste a YouTube link.
  2. Let OpusClip generate suggested clips and pick your favorites.
  3. Adjust captions/format if needed, then export for social platforms.

Strengths: speed, hooks, captions

Limitations: less control than manual editing
