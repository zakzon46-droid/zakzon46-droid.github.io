Zapier – Connect AI tools to your business apps

Automation · No‑code · Integrations

Zapier lets you connect AI tools (ChatGPT, email, CRMs, forms, social platforms) without writing code. You build “Zaps” that move data and trigger actions automatically.[web:243][web:248]

For example, a Zap can take new form submissions, send them to ChatGPT for enrichment, and then push the results into your CRM or Google Sheets.

Visit Zapier

Key strengths

  • Huge app library: Thousands of integrations for marketing, sales, productivity, and AI tools.[web:243]
  • No‑code builder: Visual editor for building workflows in minutes.
  • Reliable scheduling: Triggers based on time, events, or webhooks.

Best use cases

  • Sending leads from forms to email campaigns and CRMs automatically.
  • Using AI to summarize or rewrite incoming messages, then routing them.
  • Syncing content and analytics between different platforms.

Simple workflow

  1. Choose a trigger app (for example, new row in Google Sheets).
  2. Add ChatGPT or another AI tool as a step to transform or enrich data.
  3. Send the result to your final app (Slack, Notion, Email, CRM, etc.).

Strengths: integrations, ease of use

Limitations: pricing based on tasks & volume
